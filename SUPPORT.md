Bug Reporting & Technical Support
If you encounter any bugs, unexpected behavior, or technical issues while utilizing the RePAST (v1.0.0) pipeline, please submit a detailed report to the Shane Taylor.
Email: taylosh@iu.edu
Subject Line: Bug Report: RePAST_v1.0.0
What to Include in Your Report
To facilitate a faster resolution and support the pipeline's "fail-soft" design philosophy, please include the following in your email:
A description of the error: What were you trying to do, and what happened instead?
Terminal Output: Copy and paste any specific error messages or tracebacks displayed in the console.
Relevant Logs: Provide the technical logs generated during the failed execution from the logs/ directory (e.g., pipeline.log, preprocessing.log, or transcription.log).
Privacy Warning: Before sending any logs or error messages, please ensure they do not contain sensitive Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as participant names or specific audio filenames, to remain compliant with the Data Privacy & Security Statement.
